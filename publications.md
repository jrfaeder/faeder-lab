## Selected Recent Publications

Below are selected publications highlighting our research areas. For the complete bibliography, please see the ORCID link above.

### Rule-Based Modeling & Methods

- **Rule-based modeling of biochemical systems with BioNetGen**
  Methods in Molecular Biology (2019)
  Overview of BioNetGen methodology and applications

- **Advanced methods for rule-based modeling and simulation of biological networks**
  Describes latest developments in network-free simulation and spatial stochastic methods

### Signal Transduction

- **Immune receptor signaling models**
  Computational models of T cell receptor, B cell receptor, and FcÎµRI signaling

- **Mechanistic modeling of signal transduction**
  Detailed mechanistic studies of receptor tyrosine kinase pathways

### Stochastic Simulation

- **Spatial stochastic simulation methods**
  Novel algorithms for simulating spatial aspects of biochemical networks

- **Network-free simulation approaches**
  Methods for simulating models too large for conventional approaches

### Model Analysis & Reduction

- **Parameter estimation and uncertainty quantification**
  Bayesian and optimization-based approaches for fitting models to data

- **Automated model reduction techniques**
  Methods for simplifying complex models while preserving essential behavior
